Welcome to the HTML JavaScript Class Riddles Page

  1. What begins with an "e" and only contains one letter?


    An envelope.

  2. What 4-letter word can be written forward, backward or upside down, and can still be read from left to right?


    NOON

  3. What can run but never walks, has a mouth but never talks, has a head but never weeps, has a bed but never sleeps?


    A river.

  4. I turn once, what is out will not get in. I turn again, what is in will not get out. What am I?


    A key.

  5. What's found on the ground but never gets dirty?


    A shadow.

  6. What occurs once in a minute, twice in a moment, and never in 1,000 years?


    The letter "M"

  7. What time is it when an elephant sits on a fence?


    Time to fix the fence.

  8. What do Alexander the Great and Winnie the Pooh have in common?


    Their middle names.

  9. Before Mt. Everest was discovered, what was the highest mountain in the world?


    Mt. Everest, it just wasn't discovered yet.

  10. What is red and smells like blue paint?


    Red paint
